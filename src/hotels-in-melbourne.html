What is the best area to stay in Melbourne?

The CBD for first-timers (everything is walkable or a tram ride away); Fitzroy for nightlife and character; St Kilda for the bay.

When is the cheapest time to visit Melbourne?

March (autumn) and the shoulder months avoid the summer-event price spikes; winter is cheapest if you don’t mind the cool.
