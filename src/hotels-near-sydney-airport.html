Hotels near Sydney Airport: frequently asked questions

Which suburb is closest to Sydney Airport?

Mascot is closest and has the most airport hotels, followed by Wolli Creek, both on the airport train line.

Is there a train from the hotels to the terminals?

Yes. The airport line links Mascot and Wolli Creek to both terminals in a few minutes, and to the CBD in about fifteen.

Do airport hotels have shuttles?

Some do. If you have a very early flight, check whether your hotel runs a terminal shuttle before you book.
