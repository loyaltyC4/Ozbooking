Hotels in Darling Harbour: frequently asked questions

Is Darling Harbour good for families?

Very. The aquarium, WILD LIFE zoo, maritime museum and Tumbalong Park playground are all walkable, with plenty of casual waterfront dining.

How far is Darling Harbour from the CBD?

It is on the western edge of the CBD, a short walk to Town Hall and Chinatown, and a light-rail or ferry ride to Circular Quay.
