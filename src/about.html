What we do

Booking a hotel shouldn't mean guessing whether you're overpaying. OzBookings searches live rates across our global supply network, shows you the direct rate next to each hotel's typical selling price, and lets you book it directly, so the savings stay with you instead of going to a middle layer.

How we compare

For every stay we pull the live direct rate and the hotel's typical selling price (as supplied by our rate provider), then surface the difference so you can see what you are saving. No inflated "was" prices, no hidden booking fees. Rates and availability are provided in real time through our supplier network.

Membership

A free OzBookings account unlocks member-only rates on eligible properties, saves your trips so you can rebook in one tap, and keeps your guest details ready at checkout. Membership is optional and free. You can always browse and book as a guest.

How bookings are fulfilled

Live inventory, pricing, and reservations are powered by LiteAPI (Nuitee), a global hotel-distribution platform connecting to leading bed banks and suppliers. When you book, your reservation is confirmed with the property through this network, and your booking reference is issued instantly.

Who we are

OzBookings is an independently operated Australian business. For anything at all, our team is one email away.
