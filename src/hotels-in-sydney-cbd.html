Hotels in Sydney CBD: frequently asked questions

Which part of Sydney CBD should I stay in?

Circular Quay and The Rocks for first-timers who want the harbour; Martin Place for business and transport; Haymarket for value and food near Central station.

Is it expensive to stay in the Sydney CBD?

It is the priciest area, but rates swing a lot by date. OzBookings shows the direct rate next to the typical price so you can see value at a glance, and Haymarket is usually the cheapest CBD pocket.

How do I get from Sydney Airport to the CBD?

The airport train reaches the CBD in about 15 minutes, and most CBD hotels are a short walk from a station.

Do CBD hotels offer free cancellation?
